Our Story

Chololoy founder Victor Hitchcock Medina’s passion for authentic Mexican flavors started in Leon, Guanajuato, Mexico.

Inspired by his grandmother’s beloved tortilla soup recipe, he developed a one-of-a-kind, all natural spice blend to help share his family’s special flavors with the rest of the world.
