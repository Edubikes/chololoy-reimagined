Victor’s Tortilla Soup

Individual Portion

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Chololoy Original Seasoning
  • 1 corn tortilla cut into strips and fried
  • 2 slices of avocado in cubes
  • 1/2 chicken breast
  • 1 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Add one tablespoon of Chololoy Original Seasoning to hot water and stir until well blended and broth begins to boil. Broth is ready.
  2. Season the chicken with Chololoy Original Seasoning and cook on low heat until the chicken is cooked and then cut into cubes.
  3. In a bowl, first place cubed chicken and cubed avocado. Next, add fried tortilla strips. Lastly, sprinkle with shredded cheese.

Michelada Shrimp

Individual Portion

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of Chololoy Original Seasoning
  • 8 oz. of peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 6 oz. (1/2 can) lager beer
  • The juice from 1 lime
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. In a nonstick skillet; add oil, garlic cloves and shrimp.
  2. When the shrimp is cooked (be sure to remove the garlic cloves before the garlic turns brown), pour the lime juice over the shrimp.
  3. In another nonstick skillet, warm the beer and blend in the 2 tablespoons of Chololoy Original Seasoning until it boils. Pour over the shrimp.
  4. Note: Serve with pico de gallo with pineapple sprinkled with Chololoy Original Seasoning.

Black Bean Soup

Individual Portion

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup cooked and drained black beans
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon Chololoy Original Seasoning
  • Garnishes: chopped cilantro, chopped onion, crumbled bacon, cooked chorizo, sour cream, avocado slices.

Instructions

  1. In a blender, puree the beans, water, and the Chololoy Original Seasoning.
  2. Transfer the bean puree to a saucepan and heat over medium heat until it comes to a boil.
  3. Pour the hot soup into a bowl and garnish with your favorites: cilantro, onion, bacon, chorizo, sour cream, and avocado.
